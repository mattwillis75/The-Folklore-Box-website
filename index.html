About the Illustrator

The Folklore Box is the studio and shop of Matt Willis, an illustrator specializing in the myths, legends, and dark history of the British Isles. Alongside his personal illustration work, Matt is the designer and co-creator of SHUCK, a collaborative zine exploring Norfolk folklore, crafted alongside Siofra Connor of the Norfolk Folklore Society and undead writer Ada Nemesis.
