Sections: Zines
Description: Shuck zine was a Kickstarter project initiated by three friends at the start of the pandemic. Overwhelmed by support, issue #1 sold like hot cakes and has been in print ever since. Each Shuck zine explores a different themes within Norfolk and British folklore, uncovering age-old, handed-down, dark and twisty tales rooted in truth? Who can tell.
Written for adults, suitable for older teens, contains creepy and gory tales.
