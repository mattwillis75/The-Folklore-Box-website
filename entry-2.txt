Text:
We got the puppets out from their storage and dusted them down for their forthcoming appearance at the Black Shuck Festical 2026.

They were all in very good condition having been stored in the loft of Dee and Simon's business unit. Minimal repairs were needed. We set them up and made sure all their operatives still fitted into the harnesses - it's been a year of good living since the last festival!

Everything worked and we had a little walk around the car park before packing them into Dee  & Simons van (we couldn't do this without them!) for their journey to the festival.

We rounded the night off with a great little D&D session, ending on a cliffhanget thanks to Francis the halfing's love of gold!
